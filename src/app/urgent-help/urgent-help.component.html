Help and Support Resources

If you or someone you know needs assistance, whether it's urgent or non-urgent, this page provides a comprehensive list of resources.

Emergency Contacts

If you are in immediate danger, these resources can offer immediate help:

National Emergency Number: call 911 Suicide Prevention Lifeline: call 988 National Domestic Violence Hotline: message 1-800-799-SAFE (7233) or text "START" to 88788

Mental Health Support

For immediate mental health crises, these hotlines are available:

Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services Administration (SAMHSA): call 1-800-662-HELP (4357) National Alliance on Mental Illness (NAMI): call 1-800-950-NAMI (6264)
